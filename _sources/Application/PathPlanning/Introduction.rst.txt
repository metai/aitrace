引言
=====================

什么是路径规划
----------------


路径规划 (:term:`Path Planning`)


常用方法
-----------------

常见的路径规划算法有基于 **几何分析** (Geometric Analysis), **回报** (Reward-based Algorithms) 的方法, 基于搜索的 **网格搜索** (Grid-based Search), **间隔搜索** (Interval-based Search)方法, 基于 **多目标优化** (Multi-Objective Optimization) 的方法, 基于 **遗传算法** (genetic algorithm), **神经网络** (Neural Network) 等人工智能算法.

book
~~~~~~~

- `Robot Motion Planning and Control <http://homepages.laas.fr/jpl/book.html>`_

software
~~~~~~~~


- `OMPL <http://ompl.kavrakilab.org/>`_ : The Open Motion Planning Library

- `OpenRAVE <http://openrave.org/>`_ : OpenRAVE provides an environment for testing, developing, and deploying motion planning algorithms in real-world robotics applications.

- `MSL <http://msl.cs.uiuc.edu/msl/>`_ : Motion Strategy Library. The Motion Strategy Library (MSL) allows easy development and testing of motion planning algorithms for a wide variety of applications.

- `MPK <https://ai.stanford.edu/~mitul/mpk/>`_ : A C++ library and toolkit for developing single- and multi-robot motion planners.
