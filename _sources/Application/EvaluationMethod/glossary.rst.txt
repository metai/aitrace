.. _glossary:

名词术语
========

.. glossary::
   :sorted:

   Precision
      查准率 (`Precision <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Precision_and_recall>`_  ),  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.  :section:numref:`Section-EvaluationMethodClassicalRetrieval`

   Recall
      查全率 (`Recall <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Precision_and_recall>`_  ),  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.

   F-measure
      F-measure (`F-measure <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Precision_and_recall>`_  ),  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.


   Predicted Positive Condition
      预测阳性条件 (`Predicted Positive Condition <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Precision_and_recall>`_ , PPC),  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.

   Jaccard Similarity Coefficient
      Jaccard 相似性系数 (`Jaccard Similarity Coefficient <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Jaccard_similarity_coefficient>`_ , JSC), 即 IoU,  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.

   Dice Coefficient
      Dice 系数 (`Dice Coefficient <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Dice_coefficient>`_ , DC),  参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Retrieval` 小节.

   Confusion Matrix
      混淆矩阵 (`Confusion Matrix <http://en.volupedia.org/wiki/Confusion_matrix>`_), 参见 :doc:`/Application/EvaluationMethod/Classical/Classification` 小节.
